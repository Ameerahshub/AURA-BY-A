Our Story

Crafting timeless elegance since 2020

Welcome to Aura by A

We believe that every piece of jewelry tells a story, and we're here to help you tell yours.

Founded in Lagos, Nigeria, Aura by A has been dedicated to creating exquisite handcrafted jewelry that combines traditional artistry with contemporary design. Each piece in our collection is carefully crafted by skilled artisans who pour their passion and expertise into every detail.

Our commitment to quality, authenticity, and customer satisfaction has made us a trusted name in the jewelry industry. We source only the finest materials and ensure that every piece meets our rigorous standards of excellence.

Our Values

Quality Craftsmanship

Customer First
